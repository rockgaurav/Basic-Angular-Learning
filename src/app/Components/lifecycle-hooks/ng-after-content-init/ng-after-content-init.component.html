ngAfterContentInit
What is an ngAfterContentInit?

ngAfterContentInit is triggered after the content (ng-content) has been projected into view. It is called only once after the first ngDoCheck.

Use: 1. When using content projection (ng-content).

2. If working on a projected content (like: DOM access, validation, ya dynamic styling).

Real Scenario: Creating a modal component, where content comes from child component.

Not Use ngAfterContentInit:
  1. Projected content will be visible, but DOM access or manipulation will not working (like @ContentChild, ElementRef or apply function)
